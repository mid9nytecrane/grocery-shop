About Meisallah Business Center

Your trusted grocery and household essentials shop in Wa, Upper West Region, Ghana.

Who We Are

Meisallah Business Center is a local grocery and household essentials store dedicated to providing fresh, high-quality, and affordable products to the Wa community and beyond. Whether you're shopping in-store or online, we make it simple, convenient, and reliable.

Our mission is to connect people with everyday essentials they can trust — from groceries and fresh produce to household items and beverages — all sourced with care and delivered with professionalism.

We’re proud to serve our customers across the Upper West Region and continually improve our services through digital innovation and community engagement.

Our Location

Conveniently located in the heart of Wa, the capital of the Upper West Region, Meisallah Business Center is easily accessible to residents across the area.
